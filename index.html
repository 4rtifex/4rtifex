About Me

Hello! I'm a professional translator and software developer with a knack for tinkering with technology. My unique background allows me to approach problems from multiple perspectives, combining linguistic precision with technical innovation.

My journey began with a fascination for languages, which eventually led me to translation work. As I helped bridge communication gaps between different cultures, I realized the potential for technology to enhance and scale these efforts. This insight drove me to learn programming, and I've been building tools at the intersection of language and technology ever since.

When I'm not translating or coding, you'll find me dismantling gadgets, experimenting with microcontrollers, or building small automation systems for my home. I believe in lifelong learning and constantly seek to expand my knowledge across disciplines.
